## Hiring Process — UAE Healthcare

### Clinical Staff (Physicians, Nurses)
```
1. Advertise: LinkedIn, DOH Sheryan job board, Bayt.com, GulfTalent
2. Screen: verify license BEFORE interview (check Sheryan — public search)
3. Interview: clinical competency + language assessment
4. Reference check: contact previous UAE employer + DataFlow history
5. Offer letter issued
6. Visa application (if outside UAE)
7. DOH/DHA license transfer to your facility (via Sheryan)
8. Malpractice insurance added (facility policy or individual)
9. Onboarding: SOPs, EMR training, clinic protocols
10. Probation: 3–6 months (UAE Labour Law Article 9)
```

**Critical:** A physician's license is tied to a specific facility in UAE. They CANNOT work at your clinic until their license is formally linked to your facility on Sheryan. Practicing without facility linkage = violation for both doctor and clinic.

### Support Staff (Receptionists, Admin)
```
1. Advertise: Indeed UAE, Bayt.com, referrals
2. Language requirement: Arabic + English (mandatory for clinic reception)
3. Skills test: typing speed, phone manner, basic insurance knowledge
4. Interview: attitude + cultural fit (UAE multicultural patient base)
5. Offer letter
6. Visa + Emirates ID process
7. Onboarding: 2 weeks minimum
```

## Employment Contracts — UAE Requirements

### Mandatory Contract Elements (Federal Decree Law No. 33 of 2021)
```
□ Employee full name + passport/Emirates ID
□ Employer name + trade license number
□ Job title + department
□ Start date
□ Contract type: Limited (fixed-term) or Unlimited
□ Probation period (max 6 months)
□ Working hours (max 48h/week standard; 45h/week Ramadan)
□ Basic salary
□ Allowances (housing, transport, medical — itemized)
□ Annual leave entitlement (minimum 30 days after 1 year)
□ Notice period (minimum 30 days)
□ Termination conditions
□ Governing law: UAE Federal Labour Law
```

**Registration:** All contracts must be registered with MOHRE (Ministry of Human Resources) via the Tawteen portal.

## UAE Labour Law — Key Rules for Clinics

### Leave Entitlements
| Leave Type | Entitlement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Annual leave | 30 days/year (after 1 year) |
| Sick leave | 15 days paid / 30 days half / 30 days unpaid |
| Maternity | 60 days (45 paid + 15 half pay) |
| Paternity | 5 days |
| Hajj leave | Once per employment (unpaid, up to 30 days) |
| Bereavement | 3–5 days depending on relationship |

### End of Service Gratuity
```
For unlimited or fixed-term contracts:
- 1–5 years: 21 days basic salary per year
- > 5 years: 30 days basic salary per year
- Maximum: 2 years' basic salary total
- Paid on: resignation (>1 year), termination without cause, end of contract
- Calculated on: basic salary only (not allowances)
```

### WPS (Wage Protection System)
- All salaries must be paid via WPS (electronic transfer through approved bank/exchange)
- Deadline: within 10 days of month end
- Penalty for non-compliance: fine + work permit block
- Platform: Use Bayzat, Gusto UAE, or direct bank WPS enrollment

## Visa Process — New Hire from Abroad

```
1. Entry permit issued (clinic as sponsor via GDRFA/ICP)
2. Employee enters UAE on entry permit
3. Medical fitness test (government-designated centre)
4. Emirates ID biometrics
5. Residence visa stamped in passport
6. Emirates ID issued (6–8 weeks)
7. Open UAE bank account
8. Enroll in WPS
Total timeline: 4–8 weeks from entry
```

**Healthcare professionals:** Add 4–8 weeks for DataFlow + DOH/DHA license before they can practice.

## Performance Management

### 90-Day Review (Probation Check)
```
□ Clinical competency observed
□ Patient satisfaction scores reviewed
□ Attendance and punctuality
□ Team integration
□ SOP compliance
□ Insurance/documentation quality
Decision: Confirm / Extend probation (max 6 months total) / Terminate
```

### Annual Review Framework
```
Clinical Quality: 40%
  - Patient satisfaction scores
  - Clinical outcomes (where measurable)
  - Documentation quality
  - CME compliance

Operational: 30%
  - Punctuality and schedule adherence
  - No-show management
  - Billing accuracy

Team & Culture: 30%
  - Colleague feedback
  - Patient complaints
  - Initiative and contribution
```
